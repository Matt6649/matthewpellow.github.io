about me

I'm Lena, a science journalist slightly obsessed with design.

I just finished a masters in Science, Health and Environmental Reporting at NYU. Right now I'm a news apps fellow at ProPublica where I do a mix of writing, designing and coding to tell news stories. Before that I was at Scientific American, writing about psychology, neuroscience and the odd quirks of the human mind, and before that I was at Wired, where I wrote about media, technology and the Defense Department's latest sci-fi projects. I make posters, blog occasionally, and am excited about experimenting with new forms of online journalism. I also like running (a lot).
